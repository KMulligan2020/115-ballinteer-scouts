Hire the den

For other Scout groups visiting Dublin and the Dublin Mountains.

115th Ballinteer’s den is available for overnight hire by other Scout groups in Scouting Ireland. A great base while you explore Dublin and the Dublin Mountains.

Location

We’re in Ballinteer, Dublin 16, on the foothills of the Dublin Mountains:

  • M50: about 5 minutes from junction 13, easy access for vans and minibuses.
  • Marlay Park: about a 5-minute walk.
  • Larch Hill (Scouting Ireland’s national campsite): about a 40-minute walk.
  • Dublin Mountains: Hell Fire Club, Ticknock and other trailheads within walking distance for day hikes.
  • Dundrum Town Centre: about 10 minutes’ walk for food, shops and buses.
  • SuperValu and Lidl across the road for supplies.
  • Buses to the city centre and Dún Laoghaire for day trips without driving.

A practical hub for meetings, kit storage, cooking and sleeping between mountain days.

The building

Renovated to a high standard and fully fire compliant. Highlights for visiting groups:

  • Fully equipped kitchen
  • Main hall with underfloor heating, projector and surround sound
  • Toilets with showers
  • Separate leaders’ space with seating and sleeping area
  • Enclosed rear garden

Why book with us

  • Run by volunteers who understand what visiting Scout groups need.
