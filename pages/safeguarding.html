Safeguarding

The safety and wellbeing of every young person in our group comes first.

115th Ballinteer Scout Group is committed to creating a safe, welcoming environment for every child and young person in our care. We follow Scouting Ireland's safeguarding policies in full.

Our commitments

  • All adult leaders in our group are Garda vetted before working with young people.
  • All leaders complete Scouting Ireland's safeguarding training, including the "On My Honour" programme.
  • We follow Scouting Ireland's ratios policy to ensure adequate adult supervision at all times.
  • We operate a code of behaviour that protects the dignity and safety of every member.
  • Any concern about a child's welfare is taken seriously and acted on promptly.

If you have a concern

If you have a concern about the welfare of a child in our group, please contact us immediately at ballinteer115scouts@gmail.com.

You can also contact Scouting Ireland's Safeguarding team directly:

  • Phone: 01 499 8014 (Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm)
  • Email: safeguarding@scouts.ie

If a child is in immediate danger, contact the Garda Síochána on 999 or 112 first.
