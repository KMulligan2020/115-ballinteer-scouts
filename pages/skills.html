Adventure skills

Hands-on learning that sticks, from your first knot to nights in the hills.

Scouting isn’t about sitting still. You’ll try real skills in a safe, supportive group, and often surprise yourself with what you can do.

  • 🗺️Maps & navigation: Read a map, use a compass, plan a route and find your way on hikes around Dublin and beyond.
  • ⛺Camping & outdoors: Tents, sleeping bags, camp cooking and looking after yourself (and your patrol) under the stars.
  • 🤝Teamwork & leadership: Games, challenges and patrol systems that build confidence, communication and responsibility.
  • 🍳Cooking & campcraft: From simple snacks to full camp meals. Hygiene, safety and tasty results.
  • 🧭First aid & safety: Basics that help you stay calm and useful if someone needs help on an outing.
  • 🌲Nature & environment: Respect for wildlife, Leave No Trace, and our green initiatives.
