Uniform guide

What to wear at 115th Ballinteer. Uniform for each section, plus how to get our group necker and woggle.

Group necker and woggle

Every member of 115th Ballinteer wears the 115th Ballinteer necker and a woggle. These are the same across all sections.

  • Necker: 115th Dublin Ballinteer necker at The Scout Shop
  • Woggle: Scouting Ireland logo woggle at The Scout Shop

New families can also ask leaders about the uniform recycling scheme for second-hand items.

Beavers (ages 6–8)

Beavers wear the Scouting Ireland turquoise Beaver jumper, plus the 115th necker and woggle.

  • Beaver jumper: Beaver jumper at The Scout Shop

Bring a water bottle every week.

Cubs (ages 9–11)

Cubs wear a custom 115th hoodie (available from your section leader team), plus the 115th necker and woggle.

  • MNC hoodie: contact the MNC leader team to order
  • TNC hoodie: contact the TNC leader team to order

Bring a water bottle every week.

Scouts (ages 12–15)

Scouts wear the Scouting Ireland Scout shirt and Scout trousers, plus the 115th necker and woggle.

  • Scout shirt: Scout shirt at The Scout Shop

Bring suitable outdoor footwear and a water bottle for hike nights.

Ventures / BVG (ages 15–17)

BVG members wear the Scouting Ireland Scout shirt, plus the 115th necker and woggle.

  • Scout shirt: Scout shirt at The Scout Shop
