Contact

We're happy to hear from families, past members and the community.

  • New members: use the join form and leaders will be in touch.
  • Den hire (other Scout groups): use the form on Hire the den.
  • Everything else: speak to a leader at the den at drop-off or collection, or use whatever channel your section already uses (welcome pack, parents' group, etc.).
  • Scouting Ireland: scouts.ie for national programme, safeguarding and resources.
  • When we meet: Sections and meeting times.
