BVG: Ballinteer Venture Group

Ventures · Thursdays · 8:00–9:30 pm · Ages 15–17 · Den in Ballinteer, Dublin 16

Welcome to BVG

Ballinteer Venture Group meets on Thursday evenings with 16 members aged 15–17.

BVG puts you in the driver's seat. Each week, two or three Ventures plan and run the meetings themselves, with the planning team rotating so everyone gets a turn. It's a step up in independence and responsibility, with real adventures to go with it. Recent trips have included a camp in Luxembourg, Camp Aurora, a night hike and a trip to Wales.

Leaders

All our leaders are Garda vetted and Scouting Ireland trained. For any questions, speak to a leader at the den or see group contact.

What we get up to

Ventures plan and run their own programme. Thursday nights cover everything from skills sessions and outdoor challenges to social events and camp planning. Outside the den, BVG takes on multi-day trips, international camps and hikes in the Dublin Mountains. The Group Camp brings all sections together.

  • Member-led meetings: Ventures plan and run each session
  • International trips and expeditions
  • Hiking and night walks in the Dublin Mountains
  • Leadership and teamwork development
