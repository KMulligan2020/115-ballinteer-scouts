TNS: Thursday Night Scouts

Scouts · Thursdays · 8:00–9:30 pm · Ages 12–15 · Den in Ballinteer, Dublin 16

Welcome to TNS

Thursday Night Scouts is one of two Scout troops at 115th Ballinteer, with 30 members aged 12–15.

Thursday nights run alongside Thursday Cubs and BVG Ventures, making it one of the busiest evenings at the den. Scouts dig into real outdoor skills: patrol work, navigation, camps and hikes on the Dublin Mountains, all planned and run by the Scouts themselves.

Leaders

TNS is led by Mark (Section Leader), Amy, Dee W, Branagh and Aoife. For any questions, speak to a leader at drop-off or collection, or see group contact.

What we get up to

Thursday evenings cover patrol activities, badge work, skills training and planning for camps and expeditions. Out of the den, TNS hikes the Dublin Mountains, takes on night walks and goes on multi-day camps that members help organise themselves. The annual Group Camp brings all sections together.

  • Hiking and night walks in the Dublin Mountains
  • Multi-day camps and expeditions
  • Leadership, first aid and bushcraft skills
  • Patrol system: Scouts plan and run activities together

Uniform and what to bring

Scouts wear the Scouting Ireland Scout shirt and trousers, plus the 115th Ballinteer necker and woggle. See the uniform guide for links to buy, and ask about the uniform recycling scheme for second-hand items. Bring suitable outdoor footwear and a water bottle for hike nights.
